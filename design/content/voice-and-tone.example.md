## Voice and tone (example)

Copy this file to `design/content/voice-and-tone.md` in your project and customize. **Generic microcopy patterns** (plain language, active voice, error phrasing) are defined in `.cursor/rules/frontend/ux-writing-and-microcopy.mdc`. This file is for **brand-specific** voice and tone decisions your team will own.

- **Goal**: Keep in-product copy clear, direct, and human.

### Voice principles

- **Plain language**: Prefer simple words over jargon.
- **Active voice**: “Save changes” instead of “Changes will be saved”.
- **Concrete**: Say exactly what will happen and what users can do next.

### Examples

- Weak: “An error occurred.”
- Strong: “We couldn’t save your changes. Try again in a few seconds.”

- Weak: “Submit”
- Strong: “Send invite”

### Tone adjustments

- **Routine actions**: Neutral and concise.
- **Errors**: Calm and specific; avoid blame or panic.
- **High-stakes flows** (billing, security): Slightly more detailed, with clear reassurance and next steps.
